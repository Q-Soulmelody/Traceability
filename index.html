About Us

Traça uses the Internet of Things and big data to trace the production of food commodities; we provide traceability solutions for consumers and enterprises; we are committed to empowering sustainable development with digital technology.

Traça guarantees consumers' right to know the food production process. We advocate responsible consumption.

Traça encourages a pollution-free food production process. We advocate responsible production.

Our Services

B2B

Traça provides professional production traceability services and product promotion services for food manufacturers.

Traça's professional traceability technology can carefully track every production process of food production, providing enterprises with a quality control approach.

Traça promotes food products that comply with green production processes, which contribute to responsible production across the industry.

B2C

Traça provides consumers with traceability information query services, private dietary advice services and healthy food e-commerce services.

Consumers can obtain relevant traceability information by scanning the QR code of food packaging or directly inquiring in the software.

Traça combines the advice of nutrition experts and big data technology to provide customers with personal dietary advice.

Traça sells green and healthy foods online that are certified by the company.
